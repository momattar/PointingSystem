📅 What is a Cycle?

A cycle is a 4-week (28-day) period during which your activity is tracked and scored. After each cycle ends, all points reset and a new cycle begins.

Example: Aug 1 – Aug 28, 2025

ℹ️ Your rank on the dashboard reflects your performance within the current active cycle only. Previous cycles are not included.
🧮 What's Measured?

Every session you are responsible for has 3 tasks. Each task is evaluated as On Time, Delayed, or Not Done.

Task On Time Delayed Not Done
Attendance +1 point +1 point, −0.5 penalty −1 penalty
Record +1 point +1 point, −0.5 penalty −1 penalty (online only)
Materials +1 point +1 point, −0.5 penalty −1 penalty
⚠️ Delayed is always better than Not Done. A delayed submission still earns you the point — it only adds a small penalty.
🚨 Extra Penalties

Beyond the per-task scoring, additional penalties apply for specific failures:

ViolationPenalty
Reminder not sent at least 24 hours before the session −3 points
Caused a problem, or failed to report a problem −5 points
📊 Score Calculation
Raw Points = Attendance + Records + Materials
Total Points = Raw Points − All Penalties
Max Points = Total Sessions × 3
Score % = (Total Points ÷ Max Points) × 100
💡
Example: 10 sessions, all on time, no penalties
Raw = 30  |  Max = 30  |  Score = 100%

10 sessions, 2 delayed attendance, 1 missed material
Raw = 30  |  Penalties = 2  |  Score = (28 ÷ 30) × 100 = 93.3%
🏆 Ranking Rules
  • Coordinators are ranked by Score %, highest to lowest.
  • If two coordinators have the same %, the one with more sessions ranks higher.
  • You must have at least 24 active sessions to appear in the ranked leaderboard.
  • Coordinators below 24 sessions appear at the bottom with a Not Enough Data badge and are not assigned a rank.
⚠️
Why 24 sessions? A coordinator who only worked a few sessions could achieve a perfect score simply because they had no opportunity to make mistakes. The threshold ensures rankings reflect consistent, sustained performance over time.

💡 Tips for a Better Score
  • Mark attendance during or right after the session — don't leave it for later.
  • Upload recordings and materials on the same day as the session.
  • Send your reminder at least 24 hours before every session — missing this costs 3 points.
  • If something goes wrong, report it immediately. Silence costs 5 points.
  • Delayed is always better than not done — even late submissions earn you the point.
  • Aim for 24+ sessions to be eligible for ranking — consistency counts.
✅ The best coordinators don't just complete tasks — they complete them on time, every time.
