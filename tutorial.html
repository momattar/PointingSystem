Coordinator System Tutorial

Understand how the ranking system works and how to improve your performance

📅 What is a "Cycle"?

A cycle is a 4-week (28-day) period where your activity is tracked. After each cycle, points reset and a new one begins.

Example: Aug 1 – Aug 28, 2025

🧮 What’s Measured?

Your score is based on 3 main tasks per session:

Task Done on Time Delayed Not Done
Attendance +1 +1, -0.5 penalty -1 penalty
Record +1 +1, -0.5 penalty -1 penalty (only if online)
Materials +1 +1, -0.5 penalty -1 penalty

🚨 Extra Penalties

  • Reminder needed – -3
  • Problem reported – -5

📊 Final Score Calculation

  • Total Points = Attendance + Record + Materials
  • Penalties are deducted
  • Performance % = (Total Points / Max Possible Points) × 100

🏅 Performance Levels

Percentage Performance
95%+Excellent
75–94%Good
60–74%Needs Improvement
< 60%Weak

📈 Track Your Progress

Visit the Dashboard to see:

  • Your current rank
  • Cycle date range
  • Breakdown of your scores

💡 Tips for a Better Score

  • Mark attendance during or right after the session
  • Upload recordings and materials on the same day
  • Communicate early if delayed
  • Avoid late reminders or mistakes
